Example

Let's imagine that we have a system that only allows access to people from 18 to 60 years of age and the system asks the user to enter his age, if the user is under 18 or over 60 years old it does not allow access and sends a message depending on the age, to those under 18 it says: "You cannot access, your age is lower than the required" and to those over 60: "You cannot access, your age is higher than the required".

Since the system must behave in the same way for all children under 18 years of age, all those ages are grouped in the same partition and the same for the other two groups. To achieve 100% coverage the test cases should cover each of the identified partitions, using at least one value from each partition for testing.
