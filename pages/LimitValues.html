What is a Limit Value?

Match and administrative limits are thresholds that determine how pairs of scored records are classified. The purpose of setting threshold values is to minimize the uncertainty in match results while limiting the number of false categorizations. A lower threshold may generate more matches, but some matches may be of lower quality. Business requirements facilitate decision making. Results may vary depending on whether a conservative or a more aggressive approach is taken to defining threshold values. Boundary values are set to tell the matching process how it should handle pairs in this range. Differences in the distribution of the pairs help determine the values.
